# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone who participates in the ORIVION community. Our goal is to foster collaboration and open discussion while ensuring that all contributors feel safe and valued.

## Respectful communication

- Treat everyone with respect and consideration, regardless of their background or viewpoint.
- Disagreement and critique are part of healthy discourse, but should be expressed in a constructive manner.
- Personal attacks, harassment, or abusive language are not acceptable.

## No discrimination or hate speech

- Discrimination, racism, sexism, or any other form of hate speech is strictly prohibited.
- Criticism of ideas, institutions, or policies is allowed, but do not target individuals or groups on the basis of race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, or other personal characteristics.

## Maintaining a constructive community

- Be open to feedback and willing to learn from others.
- Acknowledge contributions with gratitude and offer help to those who need it.
- If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the maintainers by opening an issue or contacting the project owner.

## Enforcement

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces within the ORIVION project. The maintainers reserve the right to remove or block any content or contributor that does not comply with these guidelines.
